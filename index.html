Built on one of oldest active Los Angeles film studio lots, Universal Studios Hollywood stands proud as the first park to be created as part of the Universal Studios theme park line that would spread across the world. The park offers a variety of thrilling attractions and entertainment such as live action-packed shows and immersive simulations. The goal of this site is to offer easily-accessible information for tourists from all around the world who might be interested in visiting the park.
