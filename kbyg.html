What should I bring?

  • Small backpack (larger backpacks not recommended)
  • Water
  • Handheld fan
  • Sunscreen
  • Poncho or extra change of clothes (for wet rides)
  • Extra battery charger
  • Card or form of electronic pay
  • Hand sanitizer or wipes
  • Light snacks

What should I know?

  • All bags will be subject to security screening before entering.
  • There is a strip mall (CityWalk) located right outside the park.
  • It is recommended to enter the park with as little as possible.
  • Smoking is not allowed anywhere in the park.
